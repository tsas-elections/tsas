Timeline & Key Dates

Official schedule and election information for students.

Key Dates

Election Timeline

  1. 20 Dec 2025 – 21 Dec 2025
    Notification & Nominations
    Filing of nominations (20 Dec 2025) — Last date for nominations (21 Dec 2025).
  2. 22 Dec 2025
    Scrutiny & Display of Valid Nominations
    Verification of nomination forms and display of valid nominations.
  3. 23 Dec 2025 – 26 Dec 2025
    Campaign Period
    Campaigning begins (23 Dec) and ends (26 Dec). Follow campaigning rules and codes of conduct.
  4. 24 Dec 2025
    Last Date for Withdrawal
    Candidates may withdraw their nominations by this date (24 Dec 2025).
  5. 25 Dec 2025
    Display of Final List of Candidates
    Final, published candidate list after withdrawals.
  6. 28 Dec 2025
    Polling, Counting & Declaration of Results
    Polling day followed by counting and official declaration of results.

Note: Update these dates in election-details.html whenever the official schedule is released.
