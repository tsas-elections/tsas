  • 28-12-2025
    1. TSAS Elections 2025 – Voting Commencement Notice
      Dear Students,
      • Online Voting will begin at 9:00 AM on 28-12-2025 and closes at 3:30 PM.
      • Students are advised to participate in the voting process only within the scheduled time.
      • E1 Students: Please check the email ID that you used during the voter registration process. The voting link will be sent only to the registered email addresses.
      • E1 students who did not register earlier will not receive the voting link.
      • E2 to E4 Students: Please click on the “Cast Your Vote” button on the official TSAS website to participate in the voting process.
      • Each student is allowed to vote only once. Duplicate or fake voting is strictly prohibited.
      • Votes submitted after the closing time will not be accepted under any circumstances.
      • The decision of the Election Committee is final.
      • No partial or intermediate results will be shared.
      • Only the final verified results will be announced.
      Let us participate responsibly and ensure a fair and transparent election process.

      Regards,
      TSAS Election Committee
  • 27-12-2025
    1. TSAS Elections 2025 – Online Voting Process
      Dear Students,
      • Online Voting Only will be conducted on the scheduled election day.
      • E1 Students: No domain mails. Only students who registered during the voter enrollment process will receive the voting link and are eligible to vote.
        Those who are not registered must register using the link below:
        E1 Students Registration Link: https://forms.gle/T88B16KcLbueD9db6
      • E1 Non-Registered Students are Not eligible for voting.
      • E2 to E4 Students: Have domain mails and will receive the official voting link through TSAS official channel and website. They are eligible to vote.
      • Vote only within the given time. Votes after the closing time will not be accepted.
      • Each student can vote only once.
      • The decision of the Election Committee is final.
      • No partial or intermediate results will be shared.
      • Only the final winning candidates will be announced after verification.
      Let us vote responsibly and ensure a fair and disciplined election process.

      Regards,
      TSAS Election Committee
