Guidelines

Student Voting Rules & Conduct

Clear, official guidance for students during elections.

Conduct

Code of Conduct

  • Respect: Maintain respectful communication with all students and staff.
  • No disruption: Avoid activities that disrupt classes or campus operations.
  • Fair campaigning: Follow rules on posters, announcements, and digital messaging.
  • Reporting: Report violations through official channels (see Contact page).
Eligibility

ELIGIBILITY & CONDITIONS

  1. The candidate must be a bonafide student of RGUKT Basar and must be pursuing Engineering Second Year (E2) or Engineering Third Year (E3) at the time of elections.
  2. The Vice President, and General Secretary may be from any year (age 18+).
  3. A candidate shall contest for only one post and shall not contest for multiple posts simultaneously.
  4. The candidate must not have any criminal record, must not have been convicted of any offence, and must not have been subjected to serious disciplinary action by the University.
  5. Every candidate must submit a signed affidavit declaring the above.
  6. Canvassing and election expenditure shall be strictly in accordance with the Code of Conduct issued by the TSAS Election Committee.
