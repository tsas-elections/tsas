Guidelines

Student Voting Rules & Conduct

Clear, official guidance for students during elections.

Voting

Rules & Eligibility

  • Eligibility: Only eligible students may vote. Confirm your status before voting.
  • ID requirements: Carry the required student identification on voting day.
  • One person, one vote: Voting more than once is not permitted.
  • Help desk: Contact the election help desk for issues or clarifications.

Update these rules in guidelines.html to match the latest official circular.

Conduct

Code of Conduct

  • Respect: Maintain respectful communication with all students and staff.
  • No disruption: Avoid activities that disrupt classes or campus operations.
  • Fair campaigning: Follow rules on posters, announcements, and digital messaging.
  • Reporting: Report violations through official channels (see Contact page).
