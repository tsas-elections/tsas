Guidelines

Student Voting Rules & Conduct

Clear, official guidance for students during elections.

Conduct

Code of Conduct

  • Respect: Maintain respectful communication with all students and staff.
  • No disruption: Avoid activities that disrupt classes or campus operations.
  • Fair campaigning: Follow rules on posters, announcements, and digital messaging.
  • Reporting: Report violations through official channels (see Contact page).
Eligibility

Eligibility & Conditions

  1. The candidate must be a bonafide student of RGUKT Basar and must be pursuing Engineering Second Year (E2) or Engineering Third Year (E3).
  2. The Vice President and General Secretary may be from any year (18+).
  3. A candidate shall contest for only one post.
  4. The candidate must not have any criminal record or serious disciplinary action.
  5. Every candidate must submit a signed affidavit.
  6. Election expenditure must comply with TSAS Election Committee rules.
Elections 2025

Code of Conduct & Election Guidelines

The TSAS Election Committee informs all students that the TSAS Student Elections will be conducted on 28-12-2025 (Sunday) from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM.

To ensure a fair, transparent, and disciplined election process, the following guidelines must be strictly followed:

  • Discipline and decorum must be maintained at all times.
  • No unfair influencing of voters is allowed.
  • Healthy and respectful campaigning is mandatory.
  • Students must cooperate with election officials.
  • Online identity verification is mandatory for voting.
  • Misbehavior or disturbance will result in strict action and disqualification.
  • Students are encouraged to vote responsibly for student welfare.

Let us conduct the elections in a democratic, respectful, and disciplined manner. Together, let us support deserving leaders who work for the welfare of the campus.

For any queries, kindly contact the TSAS Election Committee.
